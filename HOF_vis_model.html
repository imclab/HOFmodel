More Rules:

  1. About 570 members of the Baseball Writers Association of America vote each year in December, with results announced in January
  2. You must appear on at least 75% of the ballots to be elected
  3. Sometime in the 1960s they started to enforce the rule that if you appear on less than 5% of ballots, you are permanently removed from future ballots.
  4. A player may appear on a maximum of 15 ballots (i.e. consecutive years) before being permanently removed.
